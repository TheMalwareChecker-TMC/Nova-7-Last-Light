Captain’s Log, Final Entry… This is Captain Orion, commanding officer of the starship Nova-7. If you’re hearing this… you’ve survived. For now. Earth is no more. It burned in silence. The invaders—cold, mechanical, relentless—descended from deep space. No warning, no demands. Just fire. Our defenses crumbled in hours. We were part of the last fleet—Task Force Horizon—scrambling to evacuate survivors from the Martian outpost. Seven brave souls stood with me aboard the Nova-7. Lieutenant Vega, our pilot—she tried to break the blockade over Titan. Took a direct hit to the cockpit… never saw it coming. Ensign Juno, comms—patched a signal through the jamming, gave us just enough time to warn Alpha Station. Her console exploded right after. Sergeant Kell, security—held the boarding drones off alone in the engine bay. Bought us time. Lost him in the blast when they breached. Dr. Lys, our medic—stayed behind in the infirmary to stabilize injured refugees. She locked the doors from the inside… Engineer Pax—my oldest friend. He rerouted core power manually to restart the jump drive. Radiation cooked him from the inside out. Scout twins, Rem & Ryn—barely 18. Volunteered to fly the decoy shuttles. Both taken out by a swarm of hunter-killers. I watched each of them fall. I promised I'd make it matter. Now it's just you and me. This ship. These guns. They think we’re broken. That they’ve already won. Let’s prove them wrong. Welcome to the resistance, Pilot. Captain Orion, out.
